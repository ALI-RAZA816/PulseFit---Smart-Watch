Technical Specifications

Premium components for peak performance

  • Display 1.4" AMOLED, 454x454 pixels
  • Battery Life Up to 14 days (7 days with always-on display)
  • Charging Magnetic fast charging (0-100% in 60 min)
  • Water Resistance 5 ATM (50 meters)
  • Sensors Optical heart rate, SpO2, accelerometer, gyroscope, ambient light
  • Connectivity Bluetooth 5.2, Wi-Fi, NFC
  • GPS Dual-band GPS, GLONASS, Galileo
  • Memory 8GB (stores up to 500 songs)
  • Compatibility iOS 12+ and Android 8+
  • Materials Aerospace aluminum, sapphire glass, silicone bands
